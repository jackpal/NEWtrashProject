Catch the Trash

This educational iPhone/iPad action game teaches you where to put your waste.

To play, simply drag the falling trash into the right bins. If you're not sure where to sort things in real life or in the game, simply check the included trash-dex.

This game was made by four girls who met at a Girls Who Code course. They learned to program in Swift, and made the game during their two weeks course.
